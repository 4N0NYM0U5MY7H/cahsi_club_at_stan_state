Events

Graph Neural Networks User Group

Apr. 29, 2021 by AWS & Nvidia

This online seminar series focuses on learning about graphs. Its goal is to bring together GNN ML researchers, system researchers/builders of GNN platforms and applied data scientists using graph datasets across different fields and organizations in order to disseminate fresh results, stimulate new ideas, and exchange best practices.

Register Today!

Recursion in JavaScript

Apr. 29, 2021 by Hack Reactor

Join us in this highly interactive lesson to solidify your understanding of Recursion in JavaScript! This workshop is for learners who are a couple of months along in their JavaScript learning journey. You should understand JavaScript syntax and fundamental concepts like arrays, objects, loops, and functions, as well as the basics of HTML.

Register Today!

Intro to APIs

Apr. 28, 2021 by Galvanize

This workshop is a perfect gateway to gaining a fundamental understanding of APIs and how to leverage them. No matter what your background experience is, if you have an interest in either building or leveraging existing APIs this workshop would be a great way to further your experience.

Register Today!

DatOS - A Data Cafecito

The data community will meet biweekly to network and discuss anything related to data. Join our Discord community to take part in the fun!

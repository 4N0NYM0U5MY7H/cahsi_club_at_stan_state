Goals

To help support our students as much as possible in terms of school, resources, and providing a social community.

Key-words: Support, Help, Community

Help increase the number of females in the field of Computer Science.

Key-words: Females, Increase, Computer Science

Build connections with our community and campus.

Key-words: Community, Campus

Inspire young minds to explore the careers in STEM!

Key-words: Inspire, STEM
